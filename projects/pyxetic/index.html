About

Auxetic structures are materials and geometries with a negative Poisson's ratio — unlike normal materials that thin when stretched, auxetics get fatter. This makes them interesting for energy absorption, medical devices, and CNC-machinable functional structures.

Pyxetic is an early-stage Python project for exploring and generating auxetic patterns, with a focus on geometries that can actually be manufactured on a CNC machine.
