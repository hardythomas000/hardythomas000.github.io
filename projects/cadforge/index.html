About

CadForge is a browser-based 3D toolpath visualizer built for CNC machinists who want to see their G-code before it hits the machine. It parses G-code with full 3D support including helical arc interpolation (G2/G3 with Z ramping).

Features include rapid/feed/CW/CCW arc color coding, OrbitControls for inspection from any angle, camera presets, a floating overlay toolbar, dark theme, and mobile-friendly layout. No install required — just serve the HTML and paste your code.
