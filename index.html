About

I'm a CAM Programmer at PWR Performance Products on the Gold Coast, Australia. By day I program Haas CNC machines with Mastercam. By night I build open-source tools that connect AI to the physical world of manufacturing.

My work sits at the intersection of CNC machining and software — from browser-based toolpath visualizers and setup sheet generators, to a full SDF-based CAD kernel that LLMs can drive through MCP tools. I believe the next generation of CAD/CAM will be agent-native: geometry authored by AI, validated by the kernel, verified by the machinist.

Everything I build is open source, single-file where possible, and designed to actually get used on the shop floor.
